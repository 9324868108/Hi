Privacy Policy

Your trust and privacy are our top priorities at Xwork.

Overview

Xwork collects only the information needed to provide and protect your experience. We do not sell your data. Your information is encrypted and secured by our 5-layer Glass Escrow system.

What We Collect

  • Name, email, and profile info you provide
  • Payment info (securely handled by Stripe)
  • Usage and device data (for security and improvement)

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data anytime
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Contact us for any privacy concern

For more details, contact privacy@xwork.com
